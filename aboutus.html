About us

Eloquence: learning skills academy

ELOQUENCE contributes a lot in polishing student's communication skills so that they are able to face all hurdles of life with confidence while attempting to express themselves in front of others to become a successful individual.

ELOQUENCE Academy encourages the students to improve not only their writing skills but also speaking skills for better interaction with others by performing different activities like group discussion, debates, speeches, role plays etc. It focuses strongly in improving their grammar and vocabulary so as to make them unique by using easy ways of teaching.

This is an ideal place for jobseekers, housewives who want to make their own identity and touch the feet of success.

Some of the distinguished features of ELOQUENCE ACADEMY

  1. A conducive environment.
  2. Professional & skilled trainers.
  3. No compromise with quality and service.
  4. Interactive and psychological teaching method.
  5. Manageable course hours and timings to suit busy lifestyle.
  6. Adequate infrastructure having digital classroom and friendly environment.

Our seccessive stories

ELOQUENCE ACADEMY had started its journey in 2015 providing its services and became successful in gaining the faith of our students in just a mere period of a year. We wish to build a legacy with the equal contribution of our academy and students.

We live in a fast changing, interconnected world that makes English Speaking a necessity base since it is the only language to be universally accepted. ELOQUENCE primarily gives the power to express strong emotions, ideas or customs in appropriate language which is understood by native of every country.

We use learner centered classrooms that focus primarily on individual students' learning. Students learning is judged by whether they achieve predetermined development objectives and enjoy activities like speech, debates, group discussions, Nukkad natak which equally expose them to real life like situations. These practices help gain the experience and proficiency to become a successful English speaker. We challenge you to develop your language skills to a high level. Our course will you develop cultural communication skills to give you confidence for your future endeavors. Our institute primarily focuses on personality development of individual to help them emerge as successful individual.

We work hard to provide a warm and welcoming atmosphere to students. Usually every institute focuses only on job professionals but the feature that makes us unique is that our special focus is not only on job professional and jobseekers but also on school going children and housewives who have the desire to build their own identity in society. We support them to fulfill their desire. I look forward to welcome you to the ELOQUENCE family and help you with this important step towards your future.
